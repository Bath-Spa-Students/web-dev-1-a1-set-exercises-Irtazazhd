Characters

  • Mario: The heroic plumber who sets out to save Princess Peach.
  • Luigi: Mario's loyal brother and sidekick.
  • Princess Peach: The beloved ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom, constantly in need of rescue.
  • Bowser: The fearsome villain and ruler of the Koopa Kingdom, constantly kidnapping Princess Peach.
