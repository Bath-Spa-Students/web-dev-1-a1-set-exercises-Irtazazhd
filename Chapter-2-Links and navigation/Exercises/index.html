Plot Overview

In "Super Mario: The Mushroom Kingdom Adventure," Mario and his trusty brother Luigi must embark on a perilous journey to rescue Princess Peach from the clutches of the evil Bowser. Along the way, they encounter a colorful cast of characters and face numerous challenges as they traverse the Mushroom Kingdom.
