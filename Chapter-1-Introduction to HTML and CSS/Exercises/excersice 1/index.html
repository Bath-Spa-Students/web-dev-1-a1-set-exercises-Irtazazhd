Kunafa Recipe

A delightful Middle Eastern dessert with layers of crunchy kunafa, melted mozzarella, and sweet syrup.

Ingredients

  • 500g kunafa dough
  • 250g unsalted butter, melted
  • 500g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup simple syrup (made with sugar and water)
  • Crushed pistachios for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Spread half of the kunafa dough in the bottom of a baking dish, then pour the melted butter over it.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the dough.
  4. Place the remaining kunafa dough on top and press gently to form an even layer.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven and pour the simple syrup over the hot kunafa.
  7. Garnish with crushed pistachios if desired.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!
